Personal Legal Notice and Terms

This notice is issued by the natural person controlling and publishing this website (the “Individual”).

1. Legal Status and Purpose

This document constitutes a formal notice and statement of position issued by the Individual. It sets out expectations, assertions of rights, and conditions relating to privacy, personal safety, and lawful interaction.

This notice is issued in alignment with applicable UK law, including:

  • UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR)
  • Data Protection Act 2018
  • Protection from Harassment Act 1997
  • Common law relating to misuse of private information

2. Privacy and Data Protection Rights

The Individual asserts all rights under UK GDPR, including rights of access, erasure, restriction, and objection.

No personal data shall be processed without a lawful, necessary, and proportionate basis.

3. Notice to Data Controllers

  • All processing must comply with UK GDPR principles
  • Unlawful or excessive processing will be challenged
  • Subject Access Requests may be issued at any time
  • Complaints may be escalated to the ICO

4. Surveillance and Monitoring

The Individual objects to intrusive surveillance practices including ANPR, facial recognition, and unjustified CCTV monitoring.

5. Misuse of Private Information

Any unjustified intrusion into private life may give rise to legal action under common law privacy principles.

6. Protection from Harassment

Any course of conduct causing distress, alarm, or intimidation may constitute harassment under the Protection from Harassment Act 1997.

7. Personal Safety and Security

The Individual reserves the right to take reasonable steps to ensure safety and to pursue legal remedies where necessary.

8. Valuation of Time and Wellbeing

Where the Individual is required to expend time responding to disputes or legal matters, such time shall be treated as compensable.

  • A significantly uplifted rate reflecting stress and disruption
  • A minimum of no less than ten (10) times UK National Minimum Wage per hour

This represents a reasonable assessment of consequential impact and may be relied upon where permitted by law.

9. Legal Cost Recovery

  • Legal costs
  • Administrative time
  • Damages for distress

10. Consent

Consent must be explicit, informed, and freely given. Silence does not constitute consent.

11. Digital and Communications Protection

Unauthorized access, monitoring, or misuse of digital identity may result in legal action.

12. Contact for Formal Notices

Formal correspondence should be directed via the designated contact method provided separately.

13. Limitation

This document does not override statutory law but may support legal arguments where applicable.

14. Declaration

This document constitutes formal notice of expectations regarding lawful conduct, privacy, and personal wellbeing.
